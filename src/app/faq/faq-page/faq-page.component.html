What is Chartologist and how does it work?

Chartologist is an indispensable tool designed to enhance your trading decisions by meticulously tracking the current market prices across a diverse range of assets. It furnishes you with clear buy or sell signals meticulously crafted through proprietary algorithms powered by machine learning. These algorithms leverage historical data to forecast the future prices of assets.

Essentially, Chartologist sifts through chart data to discern meaningful patterns emerging from the chaos of market dynamics. When a pattern recurs with sufficient frequency, it signifies a potential price movement with a discernible level of confidence.

Why can I only run Backtesting on a relatively recent time period?

To unleash its prowess, Chartologist must undergo training on historical data distinct from the backtesting period.
This precaution is essential as the backtesting period serves to assess the algorithm's performance. Employing identical datasets for both training and testing would yield an overly optimistic appraisal of the algorithm's efficacy.

Consequently, the training period concludes prior to the commencement of the backtesting period, with the latter being adjusted each time the algorithm undergoes retraining.

What is Chartologist Trading Bot and how can it help me?

The Trading Bot offers a convenient way to streamline aspects of your trading activities by harnessing the real-time signals generated by Chartologist. You can customize it to execute buy or sell orders automatically in response to Chartologist's signals, as well as set parameters to trigger automatic position closures when asset prices reach specific levels.

However, it's important to note that the Trading Bot complements rather than replaces your own judgment, and should be used judiciously. Monitoring its operations and being prepared to intervene when needed is advisable.
